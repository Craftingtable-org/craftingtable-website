Best Custom Items Plugin for Minecraft (2026): ItemsAdder vs Nexo vs Oraxen

By Craftingtable Staff Plugin comparison April 2026

Looking for the best custom items plugin for Minecraft? Whether you're running a Skyblock, Prison, SMP, or RPG server, custom models and textures are essential for standing out.

The problem: there’s no single “perfect” solution. Most server owners end up choosing between ItemsAdder, Nexo, or Oraxen.

This guide breaks down performance, ease of use, features, and asset protection — so you can choose the right setup for your server in 2026.

COMPARISON
The Contenders

All three options allow you to add custom items, textures, models, and blocks into Minecraft — but the way they handle performance, scalability, and workflow is completely different.

IA
ItemsAdder
by LoneDev — all-in-one content system
Popular choice
Pros
  • Complete system for custom items, blocks, furniture, and GUIs
  • Large community with pre-made Minecraft resource packs
  • Built-in hosting and automatic resource pack generation
  • Ideal for content-heavy RPG servers
  • Well-established and widely supported
Cons
  • Performance-heavy on large networks
  • Complex setup for beginners
  • Overkill for simple custom item use cases
  • Harder to optimize for competitive gameplay
  • Less focus on asset protection
NX
Nexo
by TeamNexo — performance-first engine
Recommended
Pros
  • Built-in resource pack encryption (protect your assets)
  • Optimized for performance and scalability
  • Clean, modern configuration system
  • Perfect for competitive Minecraft servers (Skyblock, Prison)
  • Actively developed and future-focused
Cons
  • Smaller ecosystem than ItemsAdder
  • Fewer pre-built assets and packs
  • Requires some technical understanding
  • Still growing in community size
  • Less beginner-friendly out of the box
OX
Oraxen
by Th0rgal — developer-friendly architecture
Balanced
Pros
  • Clean and modular plugin architecture
  • Good performance compared to ItemsAdder
  • Flexible for custom systems and developers
  • Well-structured configs
  • No unnecessary features or bloat
Cons
  • No built-in asset encryption
  • Smaller community than ItemsAdder
  • Requires manual setup
  • Less beginner-friendly
  • Fewer ready-made resources

BEST CHOICE
Which custom items plugin should you use?

If you're searching for the best Minecraft custom items plugin, your choice depends on your server type:

Nexo is the best overall choice for most servers in 2026. It delivers the best balance of performance, scalability, and — most importantly — built-in resource pack encryption. If you're investing in custom textures or selling assets, protecting them is critical — and Nexo is one of the only plugins that solves that properly.
Final verdict

For SEO, performance, and long-term scalability, Nexo is the smartest investment. ItemsAdder is still great for content-heavy servers, and Oraxen works well for developers — but if you want a modern, optimized, and secure setup, Nexo is ahead of the curve.
